5. [PSYCHOACOUSTIC TENSION VECTORS]
================================================================================
- PITCH ESCALATION: As the sequence gets longer within a single round, the underlying background drone (if present) slowly pitches up, creating a Shepard Tone illusion of constantly rising tension.
- AUDITORY MASKING AVOIDANCE: The prompt tones are synthesized with sharp transients (high initial attack) specifically so they cut through the ambient noise of an arcade. This ensures the player cannot blame a failure on "not hearing" the cue.
- THE "SLOT MACHINE" ILLUSION: Despite being a pure memory game, the audio palette is heavily borrowed from traditional gambling machines (bells, coin clinks, mechanical whirrs). This leverages pre-existing psychological conditioning associated with gambling, making the sudden-death failure state feel more frustrating and the successes feel more rewarding, driving coin-in behavior.
